Plugin Goals

I am tired of the bloat modern plugins have, and I bet you are too. All my plugins aim to meet these goals.

Lightweight

Tried of plugins taking up space, creating huge databases, or giant configs? My plugins are as small as possible with only essential code and no tracking.

Open Source

All my plugins are fully open source for anyone to use.

Lag Free

My plugins replace slow and unnessary datapacks that can hurt TPS.

Drag and Drop

Plugins should be simple addons to your game and shouldn't require getting a degree in reading configs. All my plugins are either drag and drop, have simple self explaining configs, and some even have web dashboards!
