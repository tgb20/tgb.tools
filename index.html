Plugins

High Quality and Simple to Use

Janus

Janus allows streamers to control their Minecraft servers whitelist using Twitch with watchtime, followage, and subscriptions.

AntiCreeperGrief

A very small and lightweight plugin designed to stop creeper grief. Creepers cannot damage the enviroment but can still hurt players.

2x ShulkerShells

When this plugin is enabled it will overwrite all shulkers to drop 2 shells on death. This will override the loot table and is not changed by looting.

DragonDrops

Allows the Ender Dragon to drop Elytra and Eggs when killed. You can enable or disable both Eggs and Elytra individually using the config file.

AntiEnderman

When this simple plugin is enabled Enderman lose their ability to pickup and place blocks. This does not change any other aspect of Endermans.

AntiGhastGrief

Ghasts can still cause damage to players but do not destroy blocks or start fires. Damage is slightly adjusted to make up for lack of explosions.
